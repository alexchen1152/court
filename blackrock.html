Exhibits Pertaining to Stephen Cohen and BlackRock

This page centralizes key exhibits from the Card Connect, LLC v. Shift4 Payments, LLC case that detail the investment negotiations with BlackRock and related discussions concerning Stephen Cohen's firm, Point72. These documents provide critical insight into the timeline of events, communication between principals, and the valuation discussions that were central to the litigation.

Key Court Record Exhibits

The following exhibits are foundational to understanding the BlackRock and Cohen-related aspects of the case. They include text messages, deposition testimony, and other filed documents.

  • Exhibit 1663: Search Term cohen blackrock

    Deposition testimony and text messages clarifying that "Cohen" refers to Steve Cohen of Point72, discussed as a potential investor in parallel with BlackRock.

  • Exhibit 1664: Search Term cohen blackrock

    Further communications linking the discussions about Stephen Cohen's firm with the primary BlackRock investment track.

  • Exhibit 890: Search Term black rock

    Evidence related to the initial communications, due diligence process, and early-stage engagement with BlackRock.

  • Exhibit 1661: Search Term stock blackrock

    Documents detailing the proposed stock and equity considerations as part of the potential investment deal with BlackRock.

  • Exhibit 1662: Search Term stock blackrock

    Additional exhibits concerning the valuation and specific stock-related terms proposed during the BlackRock discussions.

  • Exhibit 1666: Search Term larry blackrock

    Contains references to "Larry," likely Larry Fink, the CEO of BlackRock, in the context of the investment negotiations.
