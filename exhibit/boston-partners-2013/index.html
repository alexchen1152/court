Case Background and Caveats:

  • This case was in the Delaware Court of Chancery: C.A. No. 2019-0734-PAF
  • It involved a dispute over a merger agreement. Card Connect (a subsidiary of Fiserv/First Data) was seeking specific performance of the merger, while Shift4 was resisting.
  • Many documents were filed under seal, but some exhibits entered into evidence at the hearing would subsequently become public as part of trial/hearings.
