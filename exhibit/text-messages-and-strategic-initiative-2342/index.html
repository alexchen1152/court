"J.D. "ISAAC" ODER, II, declares and states as follows:

  1. I am over the age of eighteen (18). I have personal knowledge of the facts in this Declaration, and if called as a witness, I could and would competently testify to them.

  2. I am the Chief Executive Officer of Shift4 Payments, LLC ("Shift4"). I have held this position since Shift4's founding.

  3. As CEO, my responsibilities for Shift4 include overseeing all of Shift4's operations, including the development of potential strategic initiatives, as well as mergers and acquisitions. Shift4's History and Its Business

  4. I have a long history in the credit card and payments industry.

  5. Shift4 provides its merchants with a "one-to-many" solution. That means that Shift4 connects with a single integration to a large number of PMS/POS systems. Shift4 also provides solutions for merchants to connect with a large number of banks and payment processors.

  6. Shift4's solutions are particularly valuable and effective because of the large number of different PMS/POS systems we are integrated with.

  7. Shift4's gateway solution is brand agnostic, meaning Shift4 works with most merchant acquirers or payment processors in the country.

  8. Shift4's long-term business model is what we refer to internally as the "Strategic Initiative".

  9. Under the Strategic Initiative, an existing payment gateway provider is acquired and all or a large part of its customers are then moved to Shift4's own merchant acquiring platform. Because the gateway is its own platform, there is less resistance in this process than with a gateway provider like CardConnect.

  10. When a merchant is moved to Shift4's merchant acquiring platform, Shift4 is paid a portion of the difference between the amount that the merchant pays for credit card acceptance (the "discount rate") and the costs that credit card companies ($0.10 per transaction + 0.11% of volume). This difference is known as "the spread".

  11. In addition to the Strategic Initiative, Shift4 has also engaged in "Traditional Conversions".

  12. "Traditional Conversions" involve transferring a merchant to Shift4's payment processing platform with the use of incentives and only occurs where the merchant's existing processing contract has expired, or will expire.
