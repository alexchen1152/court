Case Background

This case involved a contractual dispute between Card Connect, LLC (a subsidiary of Fiserv) and Shift4 Payments, LLC. The core issues revolved around non-compete agreements, alleged breaches of contract, and tortious interference. A key part of the case involved communications and actions related to Shift4's development of its own payment processing system and its interactions with former Card Connect employees.
