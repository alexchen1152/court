Summary and Key Findings Related to Text Messages and Contact Cards:

  • Text Messages: Several exhibits contain explicit transcripts or screenshots of text message exchanges. Key individuals involved in these text exchanges include J. David Oder (J.D.), R.J. Bucacci, Ryan Dobnak, and T.J. Beyerman. The content of these messages reveals discussions about creating marketing campaigns targeting Shift4's alleged PCI non-compliance and system outages, with the intention of attracting Shift4's clients and agents to Card Connect.

    • Doc 231-3 (Deposition of J. David Oder): Contains a detailed reading of text messages between J. David Oder and T.J. Beyerman, discussing a campaign regarding Shift4's PCI non-compliance.
    • Doc 231-9 (Exhibit 18): Contains text messages between R.J. Bucacci and J.D. Oder, regarding "going after" Shift4 and sending a text message "to all of our agents" to send messages about shift4 problems.
    • Doc 234-4 (Exhibit 3): Screenshots of text messages are present, Sent by Dobnak to Oder.
    • Doc 234-5 (Exhibit 4): Screenshots of text messages by Beyerman to Oder.
    • Doc 248-2(Exhibit Q): Deposition of Dobnak that confirms information in exhibit 3.
      • Doc 250-2(Exhibit B): Deposition of R.J. Bucacci, confirms texts sent in Exhibit 18.
