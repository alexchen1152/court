Summary of Findings and Important Context:

  • Vulgar Language Use: The text messages and deposition testimony confirm the use of words like "motherfucker," "shit," "shitter," and "dick" in communications between J. Robbins and John Oder.
  • "I'm going to bury you": Robbins claims Oder said this to him at the holiday party. Oder denies saying this specifically but admits to saying, "I heard you resigned, you motherfucker."
  • Context of Communication: The defense argues that the vulgar language was part of the normal, informal communication style between Robbins and Oder, and did not constitute a genuine threat or intimidation. Robbins' deposition testimony supports this to some extent, as he repeatedly states that "It's Mr. Oder being Mr. Oder" in response to questions about whether the language was intimidating.
  • Text Messages: The text messages presented are mostly professional, dealing with business matters. The vulgar language is present but infrequent, fitting into the conversational and blunt style characterized in the depositions.
