Identifying Relevant Exhibits (The Challenge)

This is where it gets very tricky without direct access to the court's full electronic filing system (PACER), and importantly, without knowing the specific context and arguments of the case. Here's why, and the process used the find the documents:

  • No Central Index of all the document words: Docket is not a search engine.
  • Redactions are Standard: Court filings, especially exhibits containing private communications or commercially sensitive information, are routinely redacted. Obtaining unredacted versions is extremely difficult and requires a specific legal justification and court order, far beyond the scope of your request.
  • "SPF" and Text Message Ambiguity:
