You may not have heard of Shift4 (/tag/shift4/) Payments, but the company has been providing merchants payment processing services since 1994. Formerly
known as Lighthouse Network, Shift4 Payments includes several different payment brands but operates them all under the same umbrella strategy. The company
transacts over $200 billion each year for over 200,000 merchants.

What Products Does Shift4 Payments Offer? (/TAG/SHIFT4/)
Although Shift4 Payments contains multiple sub-brands, we'll focus on the five primary payment products for this article:

/ DOLLARS ON THE NET. This is Shift4 Payment's flagship product. It's a payment gateway
(https://coincentral.com/what-is-a-payment-gateway/) with support for EMV, contactless, and mobile payments. It also includes tokenization,
point-to-point encryption, fraud prevention, and a host of other merchant services.
/ It's Pay Bureau (itspaybureau.com). A bureau service for payment facilitators and other
merchant services providers.
/ Marketplace. A curated list of third-party point-of-sale systems that integrate with Shift4
Payments processing.
/ Skytab. A relatively new mobile payment solution with pay-at-the-table functionality.
Shift4 Payments acquired Skytab in October 2020.
/ Shift4Shop. Again, another recent acquisition. Shift4 Payments obtained the e-commerce
platform, formerly 3dcart, in November 2020.

want to pay. Beyond that, it has several additional features that you may be interested in.

Key Features

/ Merchant Services. Beyond traditional payment processing, Shift4 Payments includes
accounting integrations, staff management software, check verification services, and loyalty
programs, to name a few.
/ Bank-agnostic. Dollars on the Net is compatible with nearly all major banks and processors
in North America. This setup gives you the freedom to shop around for the best rates and
switch providers if necessary.
/Pre/Post-settlement Auditing. Before settling each batch of payments, Dollars on the Net
audits each transaction, searching for any warning signs. It also reviews previous settlements
for any irregularities after the fact.
/ Fraud Prevention. Shift4 Payments boasts several proprietary fraud prevention tools
including tokenization and point-to-point encryption.

Security

With over 20 years in the payment processing industry, Shift4 Payments (https://coincentral.com/tag/shift4/) has had an ample amount of time to perfect its security
strategy - an essential component of any payment processor. The company uses two proprietary technologies, TrueTokenization and 4Go, to keep customer payment
data secure.

TrueTokenization

Because Shift4 Payments invented tokenization, they feel that it's appropriate for them also to be the ones to trademark the term. Therefore, TrueTokenization.
With TrueTokenization, merchants replace each piece of customer payment data they collect with a unique, alphanumeric token. Because the tokens serve no
actual value, they're useless to any schemers who may get their hands on them.
