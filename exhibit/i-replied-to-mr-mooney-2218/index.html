Key Points and Observations

  • Purposeful Selection: The text messages are selected and presented by J.D. Oder II to support his narrative. They are not a complete or unbiased record of all communications.
  • Late-Night Texts: The frequency of late-night texts from Rumberg is emphasized.
  • "No Earn-Out" Messaging: Oder highlights Rumberg's statements about managing expectations, both internally and externally, about a minimal or no earn-out.
  • Vegas Trip: The Vegas trip, and Rumberg's apparent enjoyment of it, are used to counter claims of friction or a negative relationship.
  • No mention of other suits, or issues. The text presented is solely dealing with subjects within.
  • No attached EXIF data displayed directly.
