but there have not been any changes to "the terms of our agreement" to my knowledge.

made is January 1, 2018.

committed to an eight-year deal without any ability to account for changes in card brand fees or other third-
party costs, and I just cannot understand why you are so upset about covering the real costs charged for
processing your accounts. In particular, any fees that were incurred after your acquisition closed are fees that
First Data will be collecting already either by means of higher interchange or passing them on to their ISOs or
merchants like you do every day.

I am always willing to find reasonable solutions, but baseless threats will not make this go any faster.
