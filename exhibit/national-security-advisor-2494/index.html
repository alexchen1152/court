Card Connect (later acquired by Fiserv) sued Shift4, alleging breach of contract and other related claims. The core issue revolved around a Referral Agreement and whether Shift4 properly terminated it and if they competed unfairly.  The case was filed in the District of Delaware, with the case number 1:19-cv-02082.  I tracked down many public court filings, including a voluminous set of exhibits related to summary judgment motions and a Daubert motion.
