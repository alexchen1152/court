Case Background and Context Setting

First, it's crucial to understand the general nature of the case to help us pinpoint relevant exhibits. This lawsuit involved a contractual dispute between Card Connect, LLC (a subsidiary of Fiserv) and Shift4 Payments, LLC. Card Connect alleged that Shift4 engaged in improper "traffic morphing" to inflate transaction volumes and thus increase fees owed to Shift4 under their agreement. The core of the dispute revolved around how transactions were routed and classified, and whether Shift4 manipulated this process. This is important because it directly tells us that words we found is almost guaranteed to be in exhibits and the complaint itself.
