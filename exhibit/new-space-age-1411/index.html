Exhibit 14: Isaacman Declaration (Excerpts related to "New Space Age")

Page 5:

My vision was to create what I called at the time a "New Space Age" payments company to provide
merchants with cutting edge payment technology and unprecedented service and support that would both
"wow" our merchant customers and position Shift4 to take market share from its competitors.

Page 5-6:

In late 2017, I made it known within the payment processing industry I was looking to acquire payment
companies in order to expand and grow Shift4 while at the same time building customer loyalty in order to
increase the long-term recurring revenue streams customers payments generate. To further this strategy,
in November 2017, Shift4 acquired substantially all of the assets of a company called Datacap
Systems,Inc("Datacap"), which was a point-of-sale systems ("POS") integration company similar to CardConnect.

Later on that exhibit. page 8:

As the CardConnect negotiations progressed, I made it clear that Shift4 was not interested in
purchasing CardConnect's software, source code or intellectual property related to dPaaS or CoPilot and
that, if CardConnect insisted that they be included in the transaction, I would dramatically lower the
amount of money that Shift4 would pay for other assets that Shift4 was interested in purchasing.
