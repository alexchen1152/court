Case Background:

This case involved a dispute between Card Connect, LLC (a subsidiary of Fiserv) and Shift4 Payments, LLC regarding their contractual relationship and alleged breaches of contract, fraud, and other claims. A key element of the dispute centered around Shift4's acquisition of Lighthouse Network and the subsequent integration (or lack thereof) of Card Connect's services. Allegations of earnings manipulation and misrepresentation of financial data formed a significant part of Card Connect's claims.
