Case Background (Briefly):

This case involved a contract dispute between Card Connect, LLC (a subsidiary of Fiserv) and Shift4 Payments, LLC. Card Connect alleged that Shift4 breached a Reseller Agreement by failing to transition merchants to Card Connect's processing platform and by actively steering merchants to Shift4's own platform. Shift4 counterclaimed, alleging various breaches by Card Connect. The case was litigated in the Delaware Court of Chancery.
