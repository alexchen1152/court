Case Background Important for Context

This case involved a contractual dispute between Card Connect, LLC (a subsidiary of Fiserv) and Shift4 Payments, LLC. Card Connect alleged that Shift4 breached their agreement and engaged in unfair competition. Many of the exhibits relate to communications (emails, text messages) between key individuals and internal documents discussing the relationship and competition.
