Accessing Court Records and PACER

Court records in the United States federal court system are primarily accessed through PACER (Public Access to Court Electronic Records). This is a paid service. To find the specific documents, you need the following:

  • Court: You need to know which federal court heard the case. A quick search reveals this case was in the United States District Court for the Eastern District of Pennsylvania.
  • Case Number: The case number is 2:19-cv-01333-MAK.
  • Docket: The docket is a chronological list of every filing in the case. Each filing (including exhibits) is assigned a docket number. We'll need to examine the docket to identify relevant exhibits.
