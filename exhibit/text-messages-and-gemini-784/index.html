The support for Amex cards on this new program is limited to those cards that are most commonly issued, which
are "dual-branded." The most common dual-branded card is a credit card that has both an American Express

include both the Amex brand and a debit brand (e.g. STAR, Pulse, NYCE, etc.)
The reason some cards are not supported and others are is determined by American Express and whether or not
they have granted specific BIN (Bank ID Number) range authority for the card to be used on a specific "alternative"
network. Typically, this includes the typical "dual-branded" cards, but we don't have any control over this, We have
to deal with what Amex has chosen to grant.
