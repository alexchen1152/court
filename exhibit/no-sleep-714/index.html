Preliminary Non-Binding Term Sheet for Strategic Transaction between
Shift4 Payments, LLC and Card Connect, LLC
This preliminary term sheet (this “Term Sheet”) summarizes the principal terms of a
possible strategic transaction (the “Transaction”) between Card Connect, LLC, a Delaware
limited liability company (“Card Connect”), and Shift4 Payments, LLC, a Nevada limited
liability company (“Shift4”).
1. Transaction: Shift4 will seek to acquire Card Connect for a price and on such terms
as set forth in this Term Sheet.
2. Consideration:
a. Shift4 will issue to Card Connect, in exchange for all the issued and
outstanding equity securities of Card Connect that are owned, directly or
indirectly, by Fiserv, Inc. (“Fiserv”), newly-issued Class C Common Units of
Shift4 (the “Class C Common Units”) representing )_]% of the fully-
diluted equity interests of Shift4, a portion of which Class C Common Units
will be subject to vesting as described in Section 2(b) below). It is intended that
this exchange will be treated as a tax-free exchange.
b. 50% of the Class C Common Units will vest immediately as of the
consummation of the Transaction (the “Closing”). The balance of the Class
C Common Units will be subject to earn-out criteria to be determined by good-
faith negotiation between the parties, but shall in any event include Shift4 and
Card Connect achieving certain mutually agreeable financial performance
milestones and shall allow for acceleration of vesting in certain
circumstances.
c, Immediately after the Closing, the Class C Common Units will automatically
convert into Class B Common Units of Shift4 (the “Class B Common Units”)
on a one-for-one basis. The rights of holders of Class B Common Units will
be identical in all respects to the rights of holders of Class A Common Units
of Shift4 (the “Class A Common Units”), except that each Class B Common
Unit will entitle the holder to ten (10) votes, as compared to each Class A
Common Unit, which will entitle the holder to one (1) vote.
3. Conditions: The consummation of the Transaction will be subject to customary
conditions for a transaction of this nature, including, without limitation, the following,
the exact terms and conditions of which shall be subject to good faith negotiations
among the parties.
a. each of Shift4 and Card Connect having completed such business, legal,
financial and accounting due diligence of the other party and its affiliates and
subsidiaries as it shall deem necessary or appropriate;
b. negotiation and execution of mutually acceptable definitive transaction
documents;
c. _ obtaining all required third party, governmental and regulatory approvals
necessary to complete the Transaction;
d. the absence of existing litigation (or the settlement thereof) between Card
Connect or any of its affiliates, on the one hand, and Shift4 or any of its
affiliates, on the other hand; and
e. compliance with all applicable laws.
