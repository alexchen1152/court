Case Background and Context:

The case Card Connect, LLC v. Shift4 Payments, LLC (and its related appeal) involved a contractual dispute between the two payment processing companies. Card Connect (a subsidiary of Fiserv) alleged that Shift4 breached their agreement, particularly relating to a "wind-down" period and the migration of merchants. Shift4 counterclaimed. The core issues revolved around the interpretation of their contract and business practices. The case was heard in the Delaware Court of Chancery and subsequently appealed to the Delaware Supreme Court.
