Case Background

The case Card Connect, LLC v. Shift4 Payments, LLC involves a dispute related to a non-compete and non-solicitation agreement. Card Connect alleges that Shift4, through its acquisition of a company called Lighthouse, violated these agreements by soliciting Card Connect's customers and employees. The exhibits are crucial pieces of evidence presented by both sides to support their claims.
