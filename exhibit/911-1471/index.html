From: Jared Isaacman Sent: Wednesday, September 11, 2019 3:55 PM To: Don Miller Subject: Fwd: Two items

Begin forwarded message:

From: Jared Isaacman Date: September 11, 2019 at 3:54:05 PM EDT To: Mike Sharrers Subject: Two items|

Mike,

Two items to discuss with CC. One is time sensitive (today) and the other is important to get resolved in the 60 days.

  1. We have a customer, that is very important to you, me and Card Connect, that we on-boarded about a year ago to the gateway. The customer has 150+ locations live, 50+ locations going live this month and 50-75+ anticipated in each of Q4, Q1 and Q2. The volume attributed to this customer is also considerable representing 100M+ in annual volume.

(continues to next page)

The customer was sold, by us and Card Connect, that we would provide the terminal solution with the gateway sale. At the time we all knew we went live with the PAX A920. I believe there were some issues in the beginning with the certification that CC and the processor ultimately corrected, but the customer has been live for many months so I don't know for sure. Nevertheless, the customer loves the terminals, site surveys at all new locations are for A920s and the pipeline is significant - to all of us.

I just learned from Taylor Lauber on my team, who came from Card Connect, that we are out of stock and unable to fulfill many orders for this customer. That is a problem and is essentially shutting down our Q3 pipeline, which impacts Shift4, you, me and Card Connect. I have no interest in introducing another terminal to this customer. I also can't "stop Q3 deployments". I told Taylor to call his previous Card Connect contacts to see if we could secure 300 terminals ASAP. Please look into this. It's important.

(continues to another page)

  1. We also need resolution, as mentioned before, on getting the gateways certified to all processors.

    • Merchant Link is certified to all major processors, which includes Elavon, Chase Paymentech, TSYS, Heartland, First Data and Worldpay.

    • Shift4 Gateway is certified to Elavon, Chase Paymentech, TSYS, Heartland and First Data. Worldpay is in progress, although I thought this was also supposed to be completed a while ago.

      We need it. Please deliver it within 60 days.

Thank you, Jared
