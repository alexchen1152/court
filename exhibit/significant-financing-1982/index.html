Case Background:

This case, filed in the Delaware Court of Chancery (Case No. 2019-0789-JRS), involved a dispute between Card Connect, LLC (a subsidiary of Fiserv) and Shift4 Payments, LLC, stemming from a proposed acquisition that did not occur. The core issues revolved around alleged breaches of contract, particularly regarding a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) and discussions related to financing a potential transaction.
