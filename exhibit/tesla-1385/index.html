Summary and Key Findings from Exhibit 23:

  • EMV and Semi-Integrated Solutions: The early messages discuss challenges and progress with EMV certification and semi-integrated solutions, particularly with Elavon.
  • The parties discuss that TSYS would be certified before Elavon.
  • Processor-Specific Issues: There are mentions of specific issues with First Data and Elavon certifications.
  • Lighthouse and Data: A key exchange confirms that transaction data cannot be accessed through Lighthouse if Shift4 is not the processor, even if Shift4 is the gateway.
  • The parties do dicuss support, and issues on a regular.
  • Tokenization: Shift4 tokens stay with Shift4, regardless of the processor used by the merchant.
  • "Missing or Invalid Tag" Error: An error message ("missing or invalid tag (4926)") is discussed, and it's determined to be a novel issue, not an expired password.
  • Tesla Conversation: A significant portion of the later messages is a personal conversation about Tesla vehicles, with JRK (Jared Isaacman) enthusiastically endorsing them.
  • The parties discuss certification of VX805 and IPP310 Devices.

The Tesla is the very last conversation and the most recent message is dated February 1, 2021.
