Key Context:

  • Card Connect, LLC v. Shift4 Payments, LLC: This was a lawsuit involving allegations of breach of contract and related claims in the payment processing industry. Card Connect (a Fiserv company) was essentially alleging that Shift4's "SkyTab" terminal program violated agreements between the parties, particularly a non-solicitation clause.
  • Jared Isaacman: CEO of Shift4 Payments.
  • Randy Sanford, Mike Russo, Taylor Green: Employees/executives at Shift4 Payments. The "+1 (484) 354-7980" number in Exhibit J is an unidentified Shift4 Employee. "Brett and his team" are not fully identified in the provided text, but context suggests they are Shift4 employees or contractors involved with SkyTab.
  • SkyTab: The terminal program at the center of the dispute. Shift4 was offering these terminals, and Card Connect alleged this violated their contract. Side Hustle- An important keyword used for the shifty nature of the program.

The exhibits provide direct evidence of internal communications at Shift4 regarding the SkyTab program, demonstrating awareness of its potential to disrupt existing relationships ("killing me with this terminal side hustle," "manage risk," "noise around it"). The surveillance image and accompanying text message show the physical handling of SkyTab equipment, suggesting a deliberate effort to deploy the terminals. The text conversations with Randy Sanford reveal the testing and deployment phases. All of this was used to establish that Shift4 actively developed and marketed SkyTab, despite the contractual agreements that Card Connect argued were being violated.
