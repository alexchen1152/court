First, it's important to understand the case a bit. Card Connect, LLC v. Shift4 Payments, LLC was a contract dispute heard in the Delaware Court of Chancery. Card Connect (later acquired by Fiserv) alleged that Shift4 breached a Reseller Agreement. Key figures in the case include:

  • Jared Isaacman: Founder and CEO of Shift4 Payments.
  • Chris Chiodo: An executive at Card Connect, later Fiserv, and a central figure in many of the communications.
  • Angelo Grecco: The card connect president,.
