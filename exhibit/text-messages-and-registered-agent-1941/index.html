Case Background:

  • The case is in the United States District Court for the Eastern District of Pennsylvania.
  • The case number is 2:20-cv-02931-PD.
  • The core dispute involves allegations of breach of contract, tortious interference, and related claims arising from a business relationship between Card Connect (a payment processor, later acquired by Fiserv) and Shift4 (also a payment processor). Shift4 acquired Card Connect's "golden nugget" in the words of Jajeh, the CEO.

NOTES TO THE CONSOLIDATED FINANCIAL STATEMENTS
For the Years Ended December 31, 2017, 2018 and 2019

1.    Nature of Business and Basis of Presentation

Nature of the Business

Shift4 Payments, Inc. (the "Company") is a holding company, incorporated on
June 5, 2017 in the State of Delaware. The principal asset of the Company is a
controlling equity interest in Shift4 Payments, LLC ("Shift4 LLC"). The Company
operates and controls all of the business and affairs and consolidATES THE
financial results of, Shift4 LLC.

Prior to the reorganization, described below, the equity interests of Shift4
LLC were owned by Searchlight Capital II PV, L.P, ("Searchlight"), J.D. Dahe
(the "Founder"), and certain members of management. On November 27, 2017,
Searchlight and the Founder contributed their Class A Units in Shift4 Payments
LLC to the Company in return for Class A common stock ("Rocker Class A Common
Stock") and Class C common stock ("Rocker Class C Common Stock").

Rocker Class A shareholders are entitled to one vote per share while Rocker
Class C shareholders are entitled to ten votes per share. On November 27, 2019,
the Company amended and restated the certificate of incorporation to reclassify
Class A Common Stock and Class C Common Stock as Class B common stock and Class
C common stock.

On November 27, 2017, Shift4 Payments, LLC issued $5,000 Class B Units to
certain option holders of Shift4 Payments, LLC. On March 1, 2018 Shift4 Payments
LLC issued an additional 19,756 Class B Units to certain members of management
for $2,173.

Collectively, these transactions are referred to as the "Reorganization." There
was no accounting impact of the reorganization on the financial statements.

Shift4 Payments, LLC, formerly known as United Card Services, Inc. was
incorporated in 1999 in the State of New Jersey and assumed its present name in
November 2017. Shift4 Payments, LLC is a multi-channel provider of secure payment
processing and technology solutions.

Principal and Registered Office

The Company's principal office is located at 2202 North Irving Street,
