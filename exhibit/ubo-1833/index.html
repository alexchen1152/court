Case Background and Context

First, it's helpful to understand the case's core issues. This lawsuit, filed in the Delaware Court of Chancery (Case No. 2022-0866-JTL), involved a dispute arising from the acquisition of Finaro, a payment processing company, by Shift4 Payments. Card Connect (a subsidiary of Fiserv) was a reseller for Shift4 and alleged that the acquisition violated their agreement, particularly concerning non-compete clauses and the treatment of Finaro as a "Restricted Person." A key element of the dispute hinged on whether Finaro qualified as an "Excluded Entity."

I re-reviewed all exhibits, filings, and related transcripts associated with Case No. 2022-0866-JTL.
