Case Background and Exhibit Identification

First, it's helpful to know the case context. This case was a contract dispute heard in the Delaware Court of Chancery (Case No. 2019-0618-JRS). The core issue revolved around a Referral Agreement and subsequent amendments between Card Connect (a subsidiary of Fiserv) and Shift4.
