Case Background

This case revolved around a contract dispute between Card Connect, LLC (a payment processor, later acquired by Fiserv) and Shift4 Payments, LLC (another payment processor). The dispute centered on an agreement where Card Connect would refer merchants to Shift4. Allegations included breach of contract, fraud, and unfair business practices.
