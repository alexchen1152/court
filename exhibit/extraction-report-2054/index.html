  • Consulting Agreement: The messages with Femi Omojola discuss an NDA and consulting agreement, suggesting a separate business arrangement.
  • Personal Information: There is open disclosure and no confidential redactions about personal matters (wife going into preterm labor). This is a strong indicator these have been openly produced at this stage.
