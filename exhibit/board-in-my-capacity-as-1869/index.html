Case Background (for context, not part of the exhibits):

The case Card Connect, LLC v. Shift4 Payments, LLC, filed in the United States District Court for the Eastern District of Pennsylvania, involved a dispute arising from an Asset Purchase Agreement (APA). Card Connect (the seller) sold certain assets to Shift4 Payments (the buyer). A key part of the agreement involved an escrow account to cover potential indemnification claims. The disagreement centered on the release of funds from this escrow account, with Card Connect alleging Shift4 improperly blocked the release.
