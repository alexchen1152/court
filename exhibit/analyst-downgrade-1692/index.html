Analyst Downgrade ‚Ä¢ On Friday, Goldman Sachs downgraded First Data from Buy to Neutral and lowered their price target from $20 to $18 ‚Ä¢ Move was largely expected (and had likely been priced in) based on GS's recent initiation report (FD was only Buy rated name in coverage) ‚Ä¢ While downgrade was expected, the key points of contention in the downgrade were:

  1. Slowdown in CardConnect growth and the ability to scale.
  2. Slower than anticipated Clover growth driven by ISV conversion.
  3. First Data hiding true economics associated with Clover as the hardware and software cost are listed as a contra-revenue.
