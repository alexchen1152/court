Understanding the Request

  1. Case Identification: We're dealing with Card Connect, LLC v. Shift4 Payments, LLC. This helps us locate the correct court records. A quick search confirms this was a case in the Eastern District of Pennsylvania (Case No. 2:17-cv-04958-MAK).
  2. Exhibit Focus: The specific request is for exhibits related to the case.
  3. Content of the Exhibits Specific focus on exhibits presented.
  4. Text Messages and "drown." The most crucial part.
    • "Text Messages": We need exhibits that contain text message conversations.
    • "drown": This is a very strong instruction. It implies presenting the complete, unaltered text of the relevant messages, with no summarization, explanation, or editing. Essentially, overwhelming the reader with the raw data. The term "drown" strongly suggests you want all text messages from all responsive exhibits.
