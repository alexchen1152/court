Summary of Findings

  • Text Messages (Exhibits G and H): The text messages between Jaja Kiedrowski (Shift4) and both J. David Oder and Nate Hirshberg reveal discussions about Shift4's acquisition of BridgePay. Key phrases include "Shift4 is going to buy them and close that office," "We are no onboarding any resellers from anyone to slow play any attrition," "We are taking our top 40 and letting the rest die on the vine," "Sorry... not Sorry. :)," and "Just like CardConnect." These conversations highlight a strategy of selectively acquiring customers and partners, leaving others behind. There's a clear indication of intentionally disadvantaging other resellers.

  • Dead Drop Resolver Email (Exhibit U): The email and attached spreadsheet document the transfer of merchant support responsibilities from SkyWire (a Fiserv company) to Shift4. The "Dead Drop Resolver" (ddr@shift4.com) email address suggests a specific process for handling these transitioned accounts.The attached spreadsheet.
