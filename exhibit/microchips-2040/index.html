Case Background and Context:

This case involved a contractual dispute and alleged trade secret misappropriation between Card Connect, LLC (a subsidiary of Fiserv) and Shift4 Payments, LLC. The core issues revolved around payment processing technology, agreements related to "Reseller" and "Referral" programs, and Shift4's development of its own payment gateway.

Page 6, Section 1.19.

"Microchip" means each of (a) an integrated circuit payment device that contains a microprocessor and is capable of performing the EMV Specifications, and (b) each integrated circuit device that is capable of performing contactless or near field communication transactions (including, but not limited to, transactions performed by mobile devices) and is approved by MasterCard and/or Visa for use in performing contactless or near field communication transactions in the Territory.

Exhibit 26 (Docket #86-26): Shift4's Responses to Card Connect's First Set of Interrogatories

INTERROGATORY NO. 11: IDENTIFY YOUR personnel with knowledge of any alleged actual or attempted reverse engineering, decompiling, or disassembling of any component of the CardConnect Gateway, CardPointe, or Bolt P2PE devices, or circumvention of any security protocols relating thereto.

RESPONSE TO INTERROGATORY NO. 11: Subject to and without waiving its General Objections, Shift4 responds as follows: Shift4 objects to this Interrogatory to the extent that it is vague, ambiguous, and overbroad. The phrases "actual or attempted" and "circumvention of security protocols" are not defined and are susceptible to multiple interpretations. The phrases "knowledge of" is also vague and ambiguous and render it impossible to provide a complete and accurate response.

Shift4 further objects to this Interrogatory because it calls for information protected by the attorney-client privilege and/or the attorney work product doctrine. Shift4 is still in the process of investigating this matter, including with its counsel, and therefore, the identification of persons with knowledge of the issues raised by this Interrogatory would reveal privileged and protected information.

Subject to and without waiving its objections, based on its investigation to date, Shift4 states that it has not reverse engineered, decompiled, or disassembled any component of the CardConnect Gateway, CardPointe, or Bolt P2PE devices, or circumvented any security protocols relating thereto.
