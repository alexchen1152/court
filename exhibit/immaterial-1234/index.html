Case Background The case involved a contractual dispute between Card Connect, LLC (a subsidiary of Fiserv) and Shift4 Payments, LLC, primarily concerning a Reseller Agreement and a Referral Agreement. The central issue was whether Shift4 had the right to terminate the Reseller Agreement and whether it had breached the Referral Agreement.
